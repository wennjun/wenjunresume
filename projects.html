Title: Proposed System- TimeTime (Employee Clock-In & Clock-Out)
 Course Module:Web Development

Technology Used: PHP, HTML, CSS, Chart.js

Project Description:

During my studies in the Web Development module, my team and I decided to create a Clock-In Clock-Out System targeted at both employees and company administrators.

On the employee side, users can record their daily work attendance by clocking in and out. They can also view their past attendance statistics, presented both graphically and in table format.

On the administrator side, company administrators have access to the attendance histories of all their employees. They can export this data in a CSV file format, which simplifies the process of record keeping and accurately calculating payroll. Administrators also have the ability to set working days for their employees.
